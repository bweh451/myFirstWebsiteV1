About

What is this all about? I'm just a simple guy who loves food, that's all. I started this website in hopes to share some of my favourite recipes of everyday food items. Recipes that, to me atleast, are the ultimate form of said everyday food items. Like I said, I'm just a simple guy. I have no professional cooking knowledge whatsoever. Just your avarage home cook. This website is made for you, yeah YOU! I see you sitting there and worrying about what your going to cook for your friends and family. Well FEAR NO MORE! Not to worry, my friend. These recipes are simple and straighforward. So simple in fact, even a caveman can do it! I truly believe that these recipes can give you some confidence in the kitchen and will make you fall in love with food and the cooking process just as I have! Now go forth and COOK!
